    • About Me

    Since I was a kid, I've loved sports. Playing sports, watching sports, reading about sports; you name it, I was into it if it was about sports. Until recently, I thought my love came from my competative personality, and while that's still true, I've realized it's more than just a competative nature.

    I love being part of a team.

    Being a part of something bigger than myself is something I relish and look forward to, whether that's winning a gold medal on the world stage, or watching an athlete receive their degree at the end of their college career. Helping someone else achieve their goals and dreams is something I've been fortunate to be apart of over the last six years as a video coach.

    I've also come to realize my love and passion for technology extends beyond the world of sports, and I want to see how I can further impact the world with my knowledge. How can my web development knowledge and skills help a company reach their intended audience? How can I streamline a workflow to help a company work more efficiently?

    I'm ready to work towards answering these questions (and more), and I hope you are someone I can help!
